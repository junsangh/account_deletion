üì± How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your WaterLife account directly from the mobile app. This ensures secure authentication and proper deletion of all your data.

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the WaterLife app on your device
  2. Make sure you're signed in to your account
  3. Navigate to Settings (gear icon)
  4. Scroll down to the Danger Zone section
  5. Tap on Delete Account
  6. Read the warning carefully and confirm your decision
  7. Confirm again when prompted
  8. Your account will be permanently deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • All your hydration logs and history
  • Your achievements and progress
  • Your subscription and billing information
  • Your reminders and preferences
  • Your referral code and rewards balance
  • All personal information and settings
  • Your account credentials

This action cannot be undone!
